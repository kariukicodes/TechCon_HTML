Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into a global event attracting thousands of participants from around the world. Each edition has showcased groundbreaking technologies and fostered collaborations that continue to shape the future.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to bring together bright minds and leading organizations to explore, discuss, and inspire technological innovations that improve lives globally. We aim to empower participants to create meaningful impact through collaboration and shared knowledge.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Renowned for her visionary work on the first computer algorithm, Dr. Lovelace inspired attendees with insights into the foundations of modern computing and the importance of imagination in innovation.

Alan Turing

A pioneer in artificial intelligence and cryptography, Turing shared groundbreaking perspectives on machine learning and problem-solving that continue to influence the industry today.

Grace Hopper

Known as the "mother of COBOL," Hopper spoke on the importance of accessible programming languages and inspired a new generation of innovators with her dedication to education and progress.
